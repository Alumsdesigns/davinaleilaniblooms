MEET DAVINA LEILANI

Raised in a vibrant creative household, Devina discovered her love for florals not in a garden—but in a craft store. What began as experimenting with faux blooms quickly blossomed into a lifelong journey rooted in design, storytelling, and sustainability.

Based in London, she has become a rising voice in modern floral artistry, known for turning artificial flowers into breathtaking, luxurious displays that rival nature itself.

Her work challenges the norms of traditional floristry by celebrating the beauty and versatility of everlasting blooms.

With a strong belief that floral design should be inclusive, sustainable, and deeply personal, she pours heart and intention into every arrangement—whether it's an intimate dinner, a high-style wedding, or a bold corporate installation. Each piece is a conversation between texture, color, and emotion—crafted not just to impress, but to connect.

Inspired by her culture, community, and the desire to leave a lighter footprint on the planet, she has dedicated her career to redefining what luxury florals can look and feel like. Beyond her design work, she leads workshops and speaks on creative sustainability, empowering new florists to bloom with both purpose and style.

Her mantra?

Flowers that last. Stories that stay. Beauty for all.

OUR TEAM

At DAVINALEILANIBLOOMS, our team is a collective of passionate creatives and logistical experts dedicated to delivering exceptional faux floral experiences. Each member brings a unique strength to the studio, working seamlessly together to ensure every design is elegant, intentional, and flawlessly executed. We value craftsmanship, client collaboration, and sustainability at every stage of our process—from concept to final bloom.

Napat Saeng

Client Experience Coordinator

Napat is the bridge between vision and reality. From initial consultation to event day logistics, she ensures every client feels heard, understood, and cared for. Her attention to detail and warm professionalism make the design journey seamless.

Kasongo Bulobo

Production Manager

Kasongo oversees the fabrication and quality of every arrangement. With a background in theatrical set design and materials science, he ensures that each piece is structurally sound, visually striking, and crafted to last.

Gulnar Mardanova

Floral Stylist & Installation Lead

Gulnar brings technical expertise and creativity to on-site installations. Known for her precision and eye for flow, she transforms spaces with grace—making sure each bloom is placed with purpose for maximum impact.
