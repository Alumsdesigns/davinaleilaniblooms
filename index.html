Master in Faux Flower Design

Davina Leilani is a master in faux floral design, renowned for creating luxurious, lifelike arrangements that blend artistry with sustainability. With an expert eye for color, texture, and composition, she transforms premium-quality faux flowers into timeless statement pieces for weddings, events, and bespoke interiors. Her designs offer the elegance of fresh blooms with the lasting beauty of handcrafted permanence—perfect for clients who seek sophistication without compromise.

The Founder

With over 20 years of expertise in luxury floral design, DAVINALEILANIBLOOMS is a globally recognised leader in crafting extraordinary faux floral experiences. Specialising in high-end weddings and private events, Devina is celebrated for creating breathtaking, everlasting floral installations that redefine elegance. Known for her meticulous eye for detail, artistic vision, and a deep understanding of spatial transformation, Devina has earned a reputation as England’s premier faux floral designer. Trusted by discerning and high–profile clientele, she brings a passion for perfection and a commitment to unforgettable beauty in every bloom.
